Traditional

  • Different regional traditional music (Isan, North, Central, South). Eg: Mor Lam (หมอลำ), Phleng Khorat, Northern folk, Kantrum.
  • Instruments: khaen, ranat, pi, sueng, salor, etc.
  • Forms / genres: court music, ceremonial music, folk songs.
  • Lyrics: often poetic, telling stories, reflecting local life, wisdom, spiritual themes.
