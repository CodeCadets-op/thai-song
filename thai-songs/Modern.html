Modern

  • Pueblo of T‑POP: pop, rock, hip-hop, R&B influences.
  • Evolution over time: from early Western-influenced rock (‘Wong shadow’ in the 60s) through bubblegum pop, to indie and modern productions.
  • Use of modern instruments, production techniques, electronic elements.
  • Topics in lyrics often include love, youth, social issues.
